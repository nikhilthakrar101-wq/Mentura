Personal Statement Tips

Writing a strong Personal Statement can massively improve your chances of receiving offers. Here are some of the most effective tips used by top students:

  • Start with a strong opening — avoid quotes; make it personal and authentic.
  • Show your motivation — explain why you chose this subject and what sparked your interest.
  • Demonstrate enthusiasm with books, podcasts, competitions, taster days, online courses, etc.
  • Show academic curiosity — include insights or reflections, not just descriptions.
  • Include relevant work experience (if applicable) and what you learned from it.
  • Explain your extracurriculars but link them to academic or personal growth.
  • Write clearly and concisely — admissions tutors read thousands of statements.
  • End with impact — summarise your motivation and long-term interest.

If you want help refining your statement, feel free to speak to a UG student or Degree Apprentice for tailored advice.
