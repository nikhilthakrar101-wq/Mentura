Online Assessment Tips

These tips are designed to help you prepare for degree apprenticeship online assessments. More content can be added anytime you want.

  • Practice numerical and logical reasoning tests.
  • Familiarise yourself with situational judgement questions.
  • Work through example video interview questions.
  • Prepare for psychometric and behavioural profiling.
  • Review your CV and motivations before assessments.
